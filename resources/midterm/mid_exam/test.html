Aries - The Ram

March 21 - April 19

Aries is ruled by Mars, the planet of action and war. Known for their boldness, impulsiveness, and confidence, Aries individuals are natural leaders who take the initiative, charging forward without hesitation. They are driven by a need to prove themselves, often eager to face challenges head-on.

Representative: Duryodhana

Duryodhana, the eldest Kaurava from the Mahabharata, exemplifies Aries' fiery nature, determined leadership, and constant striving to assert his power. The Mahabharata recounts the war between the Pandavas and Kauravas, where Duryodhana's desire to become king of Hastinapura drove him to act with ruthless ambition. Aries is known for charging forward regardless of the consequences, and Duryodhana's relentless pursuit of power—whether through diplomacy or warfare—mirrors this impulsive drive. His bravery, self-confidence, and willingness to sacrifice everything for his cause align perfectly with Aries' boldness and determination to succeed.

“I like victory!! In other words, I can't stop drooling at gambles I can absolutely win!”

- Duryodhana, FGO - dialoge: "Something you Like"





Taurus - The Bull

April 20 - May 20

Taurus is ruled by Venus and represents stability, practicality, and luxury. Known for their reliable, persistent, and stubborn nature, Tauruses are driven by a desire for comfort and security. They are hardworking and value the rewards of their labor.

Representative: Mandricardo

Mandricardo, a knight from the Orlando Furioso epic poem, embodies Taurus' steadfast determination and unyielding nature. As a proud warrior seeking to avenge his father's death, Mandricardo is both resilient and driven by a sense of duty—traits that strongly align with Taurus' loyalty to their goals. Taurus is also known for their practical, no-nonsense approach, and Mandricardo's focus on honor and unwavering resolve reflects this grounded and tenacious nature. His desire for recognition and the stability of his legacy mirror Taurus' need for security and rewards for hard work.

“I hold no sword, yet I still carry a blade. The glorious sword...unbroken and unrivaled...come to me, even if only for a moment! Serment de Durendal!”

- Mandricardo, FGO - Noble Phantasm





Gemini - The Twins

May 21 - June 20

Gemini is ruled by Mercury and represents duality, intellect, and adaptability. Known for their quick wit, curiosity, and versatility, Geminis embody both sides of every coin—dueling personalities, shifting thoughts, and vibrant conversations.

Representative: Dioscuri (Castor & Pollux)

Castor and Pollux, the Dioscuri twins from Greek and Roman mythology, perfectly capture Gemini's duality and versatility. Born to Zeus and Leda, the twins were known for their bravery and inseparable bond, making them symbols of unity and strength. Their dynamic nature, constantly shifting roles and lives, embodies Gemini's adaptability and ability to navigate multiple identities. Gemini is often seen as two sides of the same person, and Castor and Pollux's ability to switch places and perform heroic feats together reflects this flexibility and fluidity. Their intertwined fates highlight Gemini's love for complexity and multiplicity.

“The Gemini? Oh, that's our Roman name, Brother. Oh that's right. It doesn't sound too bad. Do you like it? I didn't say I liked it.”

- Dioscuri, FGO - Dialogue 4





Cancer - The Crab

June 21 - July 22

Cancer is ruled by the Moon and represents emotion, nurturing, and protectiveness. Known for their deep empathy, Cancer individuals are often very loyal and protective, putting the needs of others before their own. They are intuitive and can be deeply emotional.

Representative: Chevalier d'Eon

Chevalier d'Eon, a French diplomat and soldier in the 18th century, reflects Cancer's loyalty, emotional depth, and willingness to protect others. As a figure who lived part of their life as a woman, d'Eon demonstrated incredible adaptability and courage, protecting French interests with unyielding devotion. Cancer is known for being deeply protective and nurturing, and d'Eon's commitment to serving their country despite personal struggles echoes this. They were constantly guided by a sense of duty, putting the welfare of others above their own—a true Cancerian trait. Their emotional intelligence and ability to navigate complex situations with sensitivity also align with Cancer's deeply intuitive and empathetic nature.

“May the Royal Family's Lily be eternal... Fleur de Lis!”

- Chevalier d'Eon, FGO - Noble Phantasm





Leo - The Lion

July 23 - August 22

Leo is ruled by the Sun, symbolizing leadership, confidence, and creativity. Known for their charismatic and dramatic personalities, Leos are natural-born leaders who seek the spotlight and thrive on recognition and admiration.

Representative: Gilgamesh

Gilgamesh, the semi-divine King of Uruk from ancient Mesopotamian mythology, perfectly embodies Leo's leadership, pride, and unwavering confidence. As a powerful ruler, Gilgamesh was determined to make his name legendary and sought to leave a lasting legacy. His journey for immortality in the Epic of Gilgamesh is a reflection of Leo's drive to be remembered and admired. Gilgamesh's charismatic nature and confidence in battle reflect Leo's desire for recognition and glory. Like Leo, he had a commanding presence and an almost divine sense of self-importance, always at the center of attention, striving to assert his dominance in every aspect of life.

“Is there any end to my treasures? Why yes, of course. But that sits right at the very limit of human knowledge. Well, one day you'll understand.”

- Gilgamesh, FGO - Bond Level 2





Virgo - The Virgin

August 23 - September 22

Virgo is ruled by Mercury, symbolizing intellect, analysis, and perfectionism. Known for their attention to detail, practicality, and methodical nature, Virgos seek to bring order and clarity to the world around them.

Representative: Sherlock Holmes

Sherlock Holmes, the brilliant detective created by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle, is the embodiment of Virgo's analytical mindset, rationality, and attention to detail. Holmes is renowned for his ability to solve complex cases through logical reasoning, a hallmark of Virgo's perfectionist and methodical nature. His ability to piece together seemingly unrelated clues and deduce the truth mirrors Virgo's intellectual curiosity and obsessive pursuit of accuracy. Sherlock's constant striving for clarity in an often chaotic world aligns with Virgo's quest to make sense of things and ensure everything is in its rightful place.

“There is nothing more deceptive than an obvious fact. The same goes for the outcome of battle.”

- Sherlock Holmes, FGO - Battle Finish 1





Libra - The Scales

September 23 - October 22

Libra is ruled by Venus, the planet of beauty, love, and harmony, and is represented by the Scales. Libras are known for their pursuit of balance, justice, and diplomacy. They value harmony in their relationships and their surroundings, and are often regarded as intellectual and fair-minded individuals who seek equilibrium in every aspect of life.

Representative: Ptolemy

Ptolemy, the ancient Greek philosopher, mathematician, and astronomer, is the perfect embodiment of Libra's quest for balance and order. Known for his Ptolemaic system, which positioned the Earth at the center of the universe, Ptolemy's work sought to understand the cosmos in a way that maintained order and rationality. Much like Libra's desire to see harmony and fairness in the world, Ptolemy's astronomical theories were an attempt to bring clarity and balance to the vastness of the universe. As a Libra, Ptolemy's intellectual pursuits perfectly align with the sign's love for knowledge and logical reasoning. He worked meticulously to develop a model of the universe that could balance the motions of the heavens and the Earth—reflecting Libra's deep respect for fairness and equilibrium. His rational approach to understanding the universe mirrors Libra's intellectualism and desire to keep things in harmony.
